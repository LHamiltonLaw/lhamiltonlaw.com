About Attorney Linda Hamilton

Linda Hamilton is a public defender-turned-immigration attorney with deep experience in courtroom advocacy and complex immigration cases. She teaches immigration law, mentors attorneys, and supervises law students. In 2024, she was awarded the Young Lawyer Pro Bono Award by the Allegheny County Bar Foundation.

Linda is licensed in Pennsylvania and authorized to represent clients in immigration matters across all 50 states and globally. She is a member of AILA, the Allegheny County Bar Association, and the Pennsylvania Bar Association.

“Every immigrant has a story worth hearing — and a future worth fighting for.”
